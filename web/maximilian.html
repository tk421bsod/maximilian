A constantly evolving Discord bot with some cool features that does some cool stuff.

Features

Changable Prefix

You can change Maximilian's prefix to prevent it from conflicting with other bots.

Music

You can play music from Youtube, with support for streams, adjustable volume, and a queue.

Reaction Roles

You can give users roles (certain sets of permissions) when they react to a message.
